About us



We had a conversation about starting an ice cream shop two years ago. with the intention of combining the freshness of ice cream with mouth-watering flavors. We discovered a fantastic location by September 2023, following the procedure of locating a site and executing a contract. We will open in March 2024 after a protracted building process to guarantee quality and safety. Many clients have welcomed us despite the fact that we were just recently created.


Our treats


SUMMER IS COMING, it is the ideal time to indulge in delectable ice cream.


Sweet Life comes in a range of tastes, from delightful sweetness to cool fruit. Visit Sweet Life to taste these amazing flavors!



We provide a large range of goods in several varieties, including gelato, frozen yogurt, soft serve, and ice cream cones. The best-selling item among them is boxed ice cream, however ice cream cones are frequently offered to patrons who dine at the restaurant. The goal of every product is to provide diners with a refreshing, cool experience.
